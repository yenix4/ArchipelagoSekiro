## Item Groups

The Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice randomizer supports a number of item group names,
which can be used in YAML options like `local_items` to refer to many items at 
once:

* **Progression:** Items that unlock locations.
* **Memories** Items dropped by major bosses to increase Attack Power.
* **Prayer Beads:** Items that increase Vitality and Posture.
* **Healing:** Items that increase Healing Gourd charges.
* **Esoteric Texts:** Items that unlock skill trees.
* **Skills:** Skills obtained as Items, such as Shinobi Medicine and Ninjutsu
  Techniques.
* **Unique:** Items that can be obtained once in a run, such as keys, notes,
  reusable items, prosthetics & unique upgrades. Doesn't include Skill Texts or 
  Skills.
* **Treasure Carp Scales:** Treasure Carp Scales.
* **Upgrade:** Non-unique upgrade materials to upgrade prosthetic tools.
* **Miscellaneous:** Generic stackable items such as sugars, coin purses, 
  mibu balloons, resistance buffs and so on.

## Custom Items

This describes the custom items added to the game to make the additional region 
blockers possible. All except for the Bell of Dispelling are used by interacting 
with the locked entryway.

* **Ashina Requisitions Whistle:** A whistle that signals for the opening of the
  gates leading to the Ashina Castle idol.
* **Abandoned Dungeon Key:** A key that opens the locked doors to the 
  Abandoned Dungeon.
* **Bell of Dispelling:** A bell that is used to lift the fog blocking the way 
  into the Sunken Valley. Must be used akin to the Illusive Hall Bell while 
  standing near the fog.
* **Senpou Temple Key:** A key used to open the locked doors to Senpou Temple.
* **Ashina Depths Key:** A key used to unlock the way into the Depths of Ashina 
  by way of the Bottomless Hole.
